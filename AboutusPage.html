About us

Koolits Frozen Yogurt Fruit Shake offers a refreshing blend of coolness and healthiness for Filipino palate. They have a wide variety of options to choose from, so you can totally make your own mix! Whether you’re a fan of classic flavors or feeling a bit adventurous, Koolits has got you covered.

Our newly opened business that is located at 54 D.M. Guevarra Corner Kanlaon, Highway Hills, right in front of the Main Gate of California Garden Square. Our convenient location makes us easily accessible for residents and visitors in the area.

At Koolits, we take pride in using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to craft our frozen yogurt and fruit shake creations. From tangy yogurt bases to refreshing fruit purees, every component is carefully selected to ensure an unforgettable taste experience. Our fruit shakes, including our signature avocado and mango varieties, are made with 100% real fruits, ensuring a burst of natural flavors in every sip.

Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always on hand to guide you through our menu and help you create your perfect treat. Whether you're in the mood for a light and refreshing snack or a indulgent dessert, we've got you covered.

We believe in promoting a healthy and active lifestyle, which is why our offerings are designed to be both delicious and nutritious. Our frozen yogurt is a good source of protein and calcium, while our fruit shakes are packed with vitamins and antioxidants.

At Koolits, we're more than just a frozen yogurt shop – we're a place where you can unwind, catch up with friends, or simply treat yourself to a well-deserved sweet escape. Come and experience the ultimate fusion of flavor and fun at Koolits Frozen Yogurt Fruit Shake today!
