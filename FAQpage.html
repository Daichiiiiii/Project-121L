FAQ

Ordering & Payment
How does the ordering system work?
To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you'd like to purchase, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your payment and shipping information to complete the order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, G-cash, PayPal, PayMaya, bank transfers, or on cash delivery. You can choose your preferred payment option during checkout.
Shipping & Delivery
How long will it take for my order to arrive?
We understand the importance of freshness when it comes to yogurt. Rest assured, we prioritize prompt delivery to ensure your order reaches you as quickly as possible. Our typical delivery timeframe is 20 minutes from the time of order placement, ensuring that you swiftly receive your freshly prepared yogurt. Please note that delivery times may vary based on your location and other factors.
Do you offer international shipping?
No, we currently do not offer international shipping. Our delivery services are available only within select cities within the country. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
